The normal distribution is defined by
\begin{align}
  1 = \int_{\mathbb{R}^n} \frac{d^nx}{(2 \pi)^{n/2}} e^{-\frac{1}{2} \bm{x} \cdot \bm{x}}.
  \label{equation}
\end{align}
where $n$ in eq.~\eqref{equation} is the dimension of argument $x$.
See more detail, \cite{Lang:alg}.
